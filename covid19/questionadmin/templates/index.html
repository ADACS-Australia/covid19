Coronavirus Symptom Checker

Your personal tracker of the symptoms of coronavirus (COVID-19)

Coronavirus (COVID-19) symptoms range from mild flu-like symptoms to pneumonia. Some people recover easily, others may get very sick very quickly.


For general advice on the Coronavirus (COVID-19) or to find out what to do if you think you have symptoms, contact your local health authority.


This app does not diagnose COVID-19, it only assesses if you have some of the possible symptoms for the infection. You will need to contact your relevant local health authority to determine if you should be tested.

Cookies Notice

We are using anonymous data from cookies to ensure that multiple responses from the same person can be easily matched. This feature is only used if you select the Tracker Survey, if you do not wish to participate, simply use Anonymous Survey link and no cookies will be used.

This website has been developed by a team of public health and IT researchers, and staff at Swinburne University of Technology, Australia. This will help determine the current and emerging COVID-19 symptoms of people across the world. This will help you improve your understanding of your health, follow changes and help us to advise health authorities about what’s going on from the community’s perspective.
